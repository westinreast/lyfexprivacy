LyfeX Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

LyfeX is a local-first health tracking application. Your data is stored locally on your device by default.

2. Information We Collect

Local Data

When using LyfeX, the following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Food logs and nutrition tracking
  • Training history and workout data
  • Body measurements and sleep data
  • Health metrics (HRV, readiness, etc.)

AI Features

When using AI features (food estimation, coaching, program generation), your health context is sent to AI providers (Google Gemini, OpenAI, xAI, or DeepSeek). This includes:

  • Recent food logs
  • Training history
  • Body metrics and profile goals

You can bring your own API keys or avoid AI features entirely.

3. Cloud Features (Optional)

If you enable cloud features:

  • Supabase: Your database can sync to private cloud storage
  • Backups: WebDAV, Google Drive, or Supabase Storage (encrypted)

You can disable all cloud features and remain fully local.

4. Data Retention

Your local data remains on your device until you delete it. We do not have access to your local data.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Disable AI features
  • Disable cloud sync
